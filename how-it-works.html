Questions

Common questions

Will this replace my front desk staff?

No — it's built to catch every call your staff can't, especially after hours, during treatments, and on weekends. Most clients keep their front desk and use NeverMiss AI as backup and overflow coverage.

Does it sound robotic?

No. The AI voice is natural and conversational, trained specifically on your medspa's services and tone. Most callers don't realize they're speaking with an AI.

What if it can't answer something?

It will gracefully take a message and notify your team immediately, or transfer the call to a live person if you'd like that option enabled.

Do I need new phone equipment?

No. We connect to your existing business number — no new hardware, no porting headaches, no downtime during setup.

How does it know my services and pricing?

During onboarding, we build a knowledge base from your service menu, pricing, hours, and policies. The AI only answers from what you've provided — it doesn't guess or make things up.

Can it actually book into my calendar system?

Yes — it connects to common booking and calendar tools. If you use something specific, tell us during setup and we'll confirm compatibility before you commit to anything.

What happens to caller information?

Call details and booking information are stored securely and used only to run your receptionist service. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
