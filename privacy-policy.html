1. Who we are

NeverMiss AI is operated by KKG Media , a business based in India . This policy explains what information we collect when you visit this website, request a demo, or use the NeverMiss AI service, and how we handle it.

2. Information we collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you give us directly — your name, business name, email address, and phone number when you book a demo, fill out a contact form, or sign up for the service.
  • Scheduling information — when you book a demo via Calendly, Calendly collects and processes your booking details under its own privacy policy.
  • Call and booking data (for active customers) — call recordings, transcripts, and appointment details processed by the AI receptionist on your business's behalf, used solely to deliver the service to you.
  • Website usage data — basic analytics such as pages visited and general location, if and when we enable an analytics tool (see our Cookie Policy).

3. How we use this information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to demo requests and inquiries
  • Set up, operate, and support the AI receptionist service for paying customers
  • Send service-related communications (appointment confirmations, account updates)
  • Improve the website and service based on aggregated usage patterns

We do not sell your information or your callers' information to third parties.

4. Caller data and your customers

If you use NeverMiss AI, callers to your business may interact with our AI receptionist. Call data is used only to deliver the service to you (e.g., booking appointments, answering questions) and is retained according to the terms agreed in your service contract. You are responsible for informing your own customers about automated call handling as required by your local laws.

5. Data sharing

We may share information with service providers who help us operate (such as scheduling, hosting, or communications tools), bound by confidentiality obligations, and only as needed to provide the service. We do not share data for advertising purposes.

6. Data retention

Call data is retained for 12 months and deleted thereafter unless you request otherwise. Contact us if you'd like your data deleted sooner.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you're located, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information (for example, under GDPR in the EU/UK or CCPA in California). To exercise these rights, contact us at kkgmedia1@gmail.com.

8. Cookies and analytics

See our Cookie Policy for details on any cookies or analytics tools used on this site.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
